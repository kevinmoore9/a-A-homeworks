Description

This original recipie by Kevin Moore is a unique twist on a classic snack that will bring you back to your playgroud days. Just like (pretty much all) mom(s) used to make.

Wikipedia

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter (creamy)
  • Jelly (Grape or Strawberry)
  • Bread

Directions

  1. Remove the bread from the loaf. Two slices should suffice
  2. With the bread on a plate (or napkin), smear a thick layer of PB across one piece
  3. Layer the other piece with a generous amount of cold jelly
  4. Smoosh the pieces of bread together, PB and J facing in
  5. Enjoy with a cold glass of milk

This is an original recipie by Kevin Moore.
